Initial Allocations

Each group who has registered for ESC and meets all requirements in the ESC constitution will be awarded $200 for the Fall semester. If the student group requires funding beyond the $200, funding requests can be submitted to the Financial Committee at their bi-monthly meetings. Each request must fall under one of the below categories. (Note: more detailed instruction on submitting requests will be covered at the Treasurers Training on Sept 6 and 7.) Also answers to some common questions can be found on our FAQ page.

-Community Building

-Competition/Build Projects

-New Projects/Initiatives

-Intersocietal

-Conferences (through leadership grant, details below)
